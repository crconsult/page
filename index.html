About Us

  • Consuldata and Research centre

    Consuldata and Research Centre (CRC) is a global institution for technical training, research and business consultancy. CRC offers research and business data consultancy services as well as technical and practical training designed for individuals and groups working in public, not for profit and private sectors. CRC Kenya has identified itself with a niche market that largely comprises private and public universities, government ministries, and Non- governmental Organizations. We have served academic institutions, international research organizations and financial institutions.

    We currently have over 1,500 studying members, in 5 countries and our students take more than 7,000 assessments each year. We have served over 500 research customers. Our current audience comprises of over 30,000 trainees who are interested in taking different courses as part of their career growth and skill development and 5,000 individuals and corporate interested in technical research support.
